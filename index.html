Frequently Asked Questions

How much time do I need to commit each month?

Most professionals spend 60-90 minutes in the studio per month with us. We handle all the heavy lifting around it.

Can I bring a client or co-host?

Yes. In fact, client stories and interviews make incredible content. We'll help you structure the conversation so it stays compliant and focused on value.

Can I use this content in paid ads?

Absolutely. Your clips are delivered in ad-ready formats. We can also indicate which segments are likely strongest for ad use.

Do you handle podcast distribution too?

We provide fully edited audio & video files plus an upload guide. If you want us to manage distribution, we can add that as an optional add-on.

I'm worried about compliance / what I can say.

We'll work with your guidelines, avoid prohibited language, and focus on education, stories, and positioning rather than promises or guarantees.
